About this apartment

A sun-drenched two-bedroom on the top floor of a quiet Ikoyi building, freshly renovated with natural materials and warm tones. The living room opens onto a private balcony with Atlantic breezes and a view over the neighbourhood canopy. Every piece of furniture was chosen by the owner, an architect who splits her time between Lagos and London.

The kitchen is fully equipped for cooking (gas hob, full-sized fridge, French press), and the second bedroom doubles as a study with a desk that faces the window. The building has 24-hour security, a generator, and a small gym on the ground floor.
